# Research: Universal Short-Heading Evidence

## Decision 1: Delete the bypass, do not replace it

**Decision**: Remove promotion/hint based solely on very-short text.

**Rationale**: No structural/form signal exists; any replacement based on capitalization, ordinal or punctuation would violate Constitution VII.

**Alternatives considered**: Tune word/character thresholds (same defect); add title lexicons (non-universal); AI inference (scope/performance expansion).

## Decision 2: Preserve existing evidence hierarchy

**Decision**: Explicit role/level/style/outline remains authoritative; supported contextual typography remains the only heuristic positive path; AI-confidence body/attribution remains protected.

**Rationale**: These are source-carried structural/form signals and already have bounded precedence.

**Alternatives considered**: Remove all recovery (would demote genuine form-backed headings); broaden typography rules (not required).

## Decision 3: Paired tests are mandatory

**Decision**: Test a no-signal body, explicit short heading, and matched form/no-form pair in both relevant modes.

**Rationale**: Proves the fix is neither permissive nor a vacuum.

**Alternatives considered**: Update only the existing expectation (insufficient positive protection); real-book literals (forbidden).
